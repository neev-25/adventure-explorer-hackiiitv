Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip?

You can book a trip directly through our website by selecting your desired adventure and completing the booking form. Alternatively, you can contact our team for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible cancellation policy. Cancellations made 30 days before the trip date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 30 days may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 6 or more people. Contact our team for group booking inquiries and customized itineraries.

What should I pack for my trip?

Each trip has a specific packing list that we provide upon booking. Generally, we recommend comfortable clothing, sturdy footwear, and any necessary personal items.

Are meals included in the trip price?

Most of our trips include daily meals as specified in the trip details. Please check the specific trip page for detailed information about included meals.

Do you offer travel insurance?

While we don't provide travel insurance directly, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance for your trip. We can provide guidance on selecting appropriate coverage.
